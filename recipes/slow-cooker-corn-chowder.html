Slow Cooker Corn Chowder

Description

Delicious creamy corn chowder with a bit of a kick! This chowder has potatoes, ham and green chilis which give it a nice spicy yet flavorful profile. Try mixing it up by adding your favourite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 cans cream corn
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cans chopped green chilis
  • 2 cups cubed potatoes
  • 2 cups cubed ham
  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 2 tablesppons butter
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions to garnish

Steps

  1. Chop green onions
  2. Combine all ingredients except green onions in a slow cooker
  3. Cover and cook on High for 4hrs or Low for 6hrs
  4. Serve and garnish with green onions
