Easy Pina Colada

Description

A very simple and easy pina colada

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice
  • 2 cups chopped pineapple
  • 1 can sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • 7 floz white rum
  • maraschino cherries

Steps

  1. Combine ice, pineapple, coconut milk and rum in a blender
  2. Blend ingredients until smooth
  3. Serve and garnish with cherries
